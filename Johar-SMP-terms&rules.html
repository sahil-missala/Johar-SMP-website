Terms and Rules of Johar SMP

1. No cheats:

Any form of cheats is prohibited and is a bannable offense. Cheats such as x-raying, hacked clients, Kill aura, fly hacking, etc are some examples of bannable offense

2. No Greifing/Stealing:

Destroying/Modifying builds is also prohibited unless the permited by builder/owner. Small griefing may be excused with a warning but repeated offenses may lead to ban.

Stealing items is also prohibited without the permission of the owner. Always ask/inform in discord what you need and are taking

Dont vandalize farms and public property, offense may lead to heavy punishment

Building explicit/Adult content based builds is also not allowed

3. PvP Rules:

All weapons are allowed except end crystals, tnt/tnt minecarts and any other explosives.

You can use explosives only outside of builds in a place with no builds

Repeated killing someone more than 2 times is also not allowed unless the other party agrees

Breaking the rules may lead to warnings or bans

4. Discord Rules:

In the Discord server dont spam messages, while there is freedom of speech please do not harrass someone or share anything explicite/adult content. Use the appropriate channels in our discord server based of the what you are disscussing

5. Ban Policy:

Minor offenses will lead to warnings or punishments

First offence will lead to 1 Day Ban

Second offence will lead to 3 Day Ban

Third offence will lead to 1 Week Ban

Fourth offence will lead to 1 Month Ban

Fifth offence will lead to Ban for the entire season

Sixth offence will lead to Permanant ban

Keep in mind the above ban duration is just for reference and ban duration may vary based on the offense/admin. If you think you are banned wrongly appeal to admin.

Attempting to find loopholes, twist or exploit rules will be treated as offence and may lead to a heavier punishment/ban
